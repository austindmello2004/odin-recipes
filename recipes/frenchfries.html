Description:

French fries are a universally loved snack made from potatoes cut into thin strips and fried until they reach a perfect balance of crispiness and tenderness. When cooked properly, the outside becomes golden and crunchy while the inside stays soft and fluffy, creating the signature texture that makes fries irresistible. Their mild, comforting flavor makes them easy to pair with all kinds of seasonings and sauces.

Beyond their classic salted form, French fries are incredibly versatile. They can be seasoned with anything from garlic and herbs to spicy paprika or Cajun blends. Some are twice-fried for extra crispiness, while others are baked or air-fried for a lighter touch. Whether served alongside burgers, sandwiches, or enjoyed alone with dipping sauces like ketchup, mayo, or aioli, French fries remain one of the most popular and universally enjoyed comfort foods.
