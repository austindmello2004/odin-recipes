Pizza Recipe

Home Burger

Description:

A pizza is a warm, inviting canvas of flavors built on a foundation of golden, oven-baked dough. Its crust can range from thin and crispy to thick and airy, but it always carries the comforting aroma of freshly baked bread. Spread across this base is a layer of vibrant tomato sauce, rich with herbs and a hint of sweetness, providing the perfect backdrop for the melted cheese that blankets every surface in gooey, bubbling warmth.

Atop this delicious base lies a world of possibility. Classic toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, and olives bring familiar, savory notes, while more adventurous combinations—such as arugula with prosciutto or figs with goat cheese—introduce gourmet twists. Whether simple or elaborate, every pizza is a harmonious blend of textures and tastes, making it a universally beloved dish that satisfies both comfort cravings and culinary creativity.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose or bread flour
  • Warm water
  • Active dry yeast or instant yeast
  • Sugar (to help activate the yeast)
  • Olive oil
  • Canned crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Garlic (fresh or powdered)
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded or fresh)
  • Optional toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, olives, sausage, basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Make the Dough
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Preheat the Oven
  4. Shape the Dough
  5. Add the Toppings
  6. Bake
  7. Serve
